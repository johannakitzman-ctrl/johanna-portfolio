Equine Nutrient Calculator

AI-powered precision nutrition for performance horses.

Click to explore ↓
Problem

Horse owners and trainers relied on guesswork or costly vet consultations to calculate nutrient requirements — a time-intensive, error-prone process with real consequences for animal health.

Solution

Built a custom AI-powered calculator that factors weight, activity level, age, and health status to generate precise, personalized nutrition plans — with a clean, non-technical UI accessible to any horse owner.

Impact

Reduced user decision time from hours to minutes. Positioned the brand as a trusted authority in equine health — driving organic search authority and return visits.

The Process

How the work works

AI is a collaborator, not a shortcut. Every tool I build, every strategy I deliver begins with a fundamentals-first framework.

01

Diagnose before prescribing

Every engagement begins with deep data archaeology — crawling analytics, auditing technical infrastructure, mapping competitive landscapes. AI accelerates the synthesis; human judgment shapes the direction.

02

Build systems, not campaigns

Individual tactics don't compound. I architect interconnected systems — content engines, automation workflows, brand frameworks — where each element reinforces the others and results accumulate over time.

03

Measure what actually matters

Vanity metrics are noise. I build custom reporting frameworks that surface the signals that drive decisions — and I use AI to turn those signals into clear, executive-ready narratives that compel action.

✦

I use Claude, v0.dev, and ChatGPT as strategic partners — for ideation, prototyping, and synthesis. Every strategy, analysis, and deliverable is shaped, validated, and owned by me.
